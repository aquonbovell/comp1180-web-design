What is Climate Change?

Climate change is a long-term shift in a place's temperature and usual weather patterns. Climate change can relate to a specific place or the entire world.

Why is Climate Change a Serious Problem?

Climate change is the greatest threat to human health in recorded history, experts warn. World Health Organization Director-General Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus says hazards presented by climate change might surpass those caused by any one illness.

Who is Most responsible?

When Will Climate Change Be Unavoidable?

If we stopped producing greenhouse gases today, global temperatures would begin to level off within a few years. However, it may not be too late to avoid or mitigate some of the worst consequences of climate change.
