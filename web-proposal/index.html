Feasibility Analysis

Concept Statement

Service

DANCS Design is an online interior design business made to be accessible to the residents of Barbados. We help you source high quality residential and commercial materials to create the room of your dreams. We offer a wide variety of products ranging from couch pillows and decorative paintings/pictures, desks, and plants. The aim is to provide high quality products while keeping up with new trends in interior design. The products will be stored in a warehouse in Barbados, delivered to consumers and will host materials from both the Caribbean and internationally.

Target Market

Situated in Barbados, this business will be mainly for individuals and companies, specifically residential and commercial customers. Our business is both for new residents and businesses, or for those that are looking to change the look and feel of their building.

Is DANCS Designs Necessary?

There is a need for interior designers with an artistic eye and a typical approach to styling the interior of buildings. DANCS Designs will provide this. Individuals will revel in our high quality and creative approach to sprucing up their building of choice.

Our Company is Special for This Reason

Besides providing a suite of products from well known locations in the United States or England. We also import high quality items from foreign language speaking countries. Many of these countries have different cultures, thus they produce an abundance of distinct and unique designs that could go straight into your home or business depending on the type of atmosphere you are trying to achieve.

Management Team

The company DANCS Designs was founded by the team Hi-tech. Its members consist of our team leader Aquon Bovell and its other members Catherine Manning, Nakeada Trotman, Dominic Ward and Stephanie Harris. They were all taught entrepreneurship by the illustrious Mr. Clinton Gittens.

Product/Service Feasibility

The Google Trends website ranks searches from a range of 0 to 100, from least popular to highest respectively. In the months September to October 2021 there was a noticeable spike in the searches for interior design. The popularity number for the searches was 97.

Industry/Target Market Feasibility

The company is centered around design, specifically interior design, and imports high quality designs from regional or international countries. Its main demographic are the many individuals who were located in St. Michael, St. James and Christ Church from the Google Trends search above. These customers are mainly going to require a fresh change to their living or work environment, or are in a new building space that needs re/furbishing.

Business Model

Our business falls under the standard business model; specifically, the traditional retailer business model as we are a business where its products are made by others and will be sold to consumers at a markup from the original price. Which also explains why this model best suits the company as the traditional retailer business model is a business model calling for a firm to sell its products or services, made by others, directly to consumers at a markup from the original price. Can be sold online or offline. (Barringer Bruce R; Ireland R Duane; Entrepreneurship - Fifth Edition) To further explain why this model best suits us. We can be compared to the online retail clothing and shoe brand Zappos,which provides products from companies like Levi’s and Adidas. Alike DANCS Designs, Zappos also falls under the category of the traditional retailer business model.

Industry Analysis

Our business can be said to fall under the fragmented industries category since there are multiple interior design businesses in Barbados and there is no one company that holds a monopoly. It is a realistic place for our business to enter due to the lack of interior design businesses in Barbados that offer as high a quality or as high a level of unconventionality as we do. Though the interior design industry can be said to be a slow-growth industry, there is a meaningful difference between our products and our competitors.
